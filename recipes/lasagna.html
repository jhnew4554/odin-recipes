lasagna

Description

Lasagna(or Lasagne) is a type of pasta,possibly one of the oldest types,made of very wide,flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with feelings such as ragu(ground meats and tomato sauce),vegetables,cheeses(which may include ricotta,mozzarella,and parmesan) ,and seasonings and spices,like italian seasoning,such as garlic,oregano and basil.

Ingredients

  • 12 whole wheat lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic,chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano,or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1(16 ounce) package cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 1/2 cups tomato-basil pasta sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozarella cheese
